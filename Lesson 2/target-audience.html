Target Audience

The target audience for this webpage will include an age range of teenagers through seniors. The majority of the audience will likely live in the area, though some may be people visiting or vacationing somewhere in the vincinity. Additional demographics will include event organizers, weather and climate enthusiasts, school teachers, and potentially local news and newspaper sources.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Persona 1 opens website to check weather info for an upcoming baseball game. He likes to use the website because it's more specific to the area and a little easier to use than the news website. He checks the "Preston" page to check on weather updates. He also checks the information from the prior year to get a better idea for weather predictions.

Scenario 2

Persona 2 opens website the day before her lesson on weather patterns. She scrolls throug the main page to look at the featured stories. She picks one that she likes to discuss with the class. She then navigates to the "Storm Center" page to look more specific information as part of a homework assignment for her class.

Scenario 3

An avid gardner and local community supporter likes to browse the page in her spare time in order to plan for her time at the community garden. She regulary likes to check the weather predictions on the her town subpage and on the "Storm Center" page.

Scenario 4

A recently retired couple finds the website through a search engine when planning their early spring/summer cruise through all of the mountain west. They cursorily examine the home page to look at any potential local events before quickly glancing at the "Storm Center" page to look for any inclement weather.

Scenario 5

A writer at the local newspaper quickly checks the website once a week for potential stories and weather-related events. He briefly scans each page to check for new content.

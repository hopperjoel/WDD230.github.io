Preparation for Independence Day Celebration

People are getting ready for the annual 4th of July Parade and Celebration. The daily events will start off with the parade at 10:00 AM on July 3rd (observed) followed by the 23rd annual BBQ Cookoff at noon. Events for the day will wrap up with the 3 on 3 basketball tournament finals at 7:00 PM and Fireworks at 10:00. All events will be held in the town square park with the parade running from 600 N to 400 S Main Street.

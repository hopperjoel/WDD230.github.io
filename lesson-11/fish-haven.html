Getting Ready for Raspberry Days

Even though Raspberry Days is a Garden City celebration, the good folks of Fish Haven are getting ready. "We always have tons of tourists this time of year. It's a good time to attract customers and have a little fun," says Kent Smith from Fish Haven. Many locals have reported that tourism is up this year, likely as a result of ending Covid-19 restrictions. Many residents are advising people to plan their visits to the Bear Lake area before or after Raspberry Days season in order to avoid overcrowding.

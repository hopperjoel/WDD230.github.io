Forest Fires Rage Across the Mountain West

This summer is turning out to be one of the hottest on record. The deadly combination of high summer temperatures and minimal winter snowfall has resulted in hundreds of fires raging all across the western United States. Over 55 large wildfires have been reported across the states covering over 400 square miles. Some fires have been reported as contained or nearly contained, but several still remain with zero containment. Tempartures around the west have averaged over 10 degrees warmer than usual with a few record-high temperatures being recorded in places like St. George at 117 degrees fahrenheit.

Scooters, ATV's, and Jeep's

Honda Motor Scooter Rentals

Our scooters are a fantastic way to get around the island! Enjoy the fresh breeze as you see the sights and explore. These scooters are perfect for the individual or couple who plan to enjoy all the Cozumel has to offer without excessively paying for taxies or relying on hotel transportation. Our half-day rental is also an excellent choice for cruise excurions.
Our scooters range from a single seat to a double.

  • Honda Metropolitan (49cc)— 1 person
  • Honda Dio (110cc)— 2 person
  • Honda PCX150 (149cc)— 2 person

ATV Side-by-Side Rentals

Our side-by-side ATV's are perfect for those who crave adventure! Explore any trail that Cozumel has to offer before cruising back to into town to enjoy the city. These ATV's offer responsive steering and handling to safely explore anything. Room and seatbelts for 4 people.

  • Honda Pioneer 1000- 4 people max

Jeep Rentals

Our Jeeps offer the best of all worlds: modified suspension and handling to tackle the toughest of trails, all the comforts and amenities you would expect from a car. Our 4-door Wranglers are big enough to handle 5 people with extra space for luggage. Our 2-door Wranglers are perfect for 2 with a little extra carrying space.
The 2-door Wrangler sports a manual transmission.

  • 4-door Jeep Wrangler—manual with A/C— 5 people max
  • 2-door Jeep Wrangler—open air—manual— 4 people max
